Beef Loin with mushroom

A simple and delicious recipe to enjoy tender meat with all the
flavor that the cream sauce gives to greeno with mushrooms.
On this occasion we used Loin (or Sirloin of Veal, as it is known in Spain)
but you can also use other cuts such as Tail of Cuadril, Ojo de Bife or even
chicken or Fish, they are also very delicious.

  • Beef Loin 850 Gr
  • Champignon 200 Gr
  • Onion 3 u. (medium)
  • Green onion 3 u.
  • Cream / Cream 200 Ml.
  • White Wine 100 Ml.
  • Butter / Butter 25 Gr.
  • Olive Oil c/n
  • Salt and pepper to taste

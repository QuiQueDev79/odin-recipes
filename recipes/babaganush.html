Baba Ganush

Recipe for Baba Ganoush

We share this recipe that we love and is ideal to serve
as a dip or spread on bread, it is also very easy and is
really delicious. As with any recipe, there may be variations,
both in ingredients and in cooking or preparation techniques,
but we assure you that with our recipe you will obtain a delicious
Babaganush that we can prepare with what we have on hand.

The traditional Baba Ganoush recipe incorporates an uncommon
ingredient in our cuisine and it is “Tahini”. Tahini is a creamy
paste made from crushed sesame seeds. This is a very common
ingredient in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine, it is used
to prepare a wide variety of dishes, from sauces to dressings and
also desserts.

Ingredients

  • Eggplants 2 u
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Tahini 2 Tbsp
  • Half Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil 4 Tbsp
  • Ground Chili 1 Tsp
  • Pinch of salt

Makes 1 Bowl of Baba Ganoush, to be enjoyed by 4 people without problem.

Preparation time

  1. Place the whole eggplants on a platter and bake for 30 minutes at high temperature (200°C)
  2. Remove from oven and let cool
  3. Open the eggplants and remove the inside with a spoon. Place in a bowl
  4. Mash with a fork until puree. It can also be processed to obtain a softer paste
  5. Add chopped garlic, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt to the bowl and mix everything well
  6. Serve with a little more olive oil on top, chopped parsley and enjoy with delicious bread
